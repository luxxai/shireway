About Us

At Shireway Tollers, we breed with purpose, care, and an eye to the future. Our dogs are raised with love in the foothills of the Rockies, where they grow into sound, capable companions—whether for the field, the ring, or the hearth. Every litter is thoughtfully planned, with temperament, type, and health at the forefront.
